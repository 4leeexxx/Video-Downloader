Free Online Video Converter — MP4, WebM, GIF, MP3 & More

Convert Video to MP4

MP4 is the most universally supported video format, compatible with every device, browser, and social media platform. Convert MOV, AVI, WebM, or MKV files to MP4 for maximum compatibility.

Convert Video to GIF

Animated GIFs are perfect for sharing short clips on social media, messaging apps, and websites. Use the trim feature to select just the best few seconds of your video before converting.
